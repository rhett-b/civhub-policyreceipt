Local Law 97

Policy Receipt

Policy Overview:

New York City's Local Law 97 is part of the Climate Mobilization Act. It requires buildings over 25,000 square feet to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 40% by 2030 and 80% by 2050. Penalties apply for non-compliance.
